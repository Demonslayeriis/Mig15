<h1>Mig 15 The Great grand father of modern  russian jets</h1>
<img src="mig.jpg.jpg">
Mig 15 was developed by  Mikoyan-Gurevich for soviet union. it s first flight was in december 30th 1947.
 2 years after its first test flight it was introduced in sovient air force. It can achieve high transonic
 speed. It was one of the most produced fighter aircraft as over 13000 units of it were built. It mainly
 fought against the f86 sabre of the united states. It was mainly in action in the korean war.
